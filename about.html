About us

SAINT-PETERSBURG TOURS specializing in quality accommodation and unparalleled personalized tour-guiding service provides tour guiding services, transportation and comfortable apartments in St. Petersburg.

Founded in 1998 by a Russian who received his high-school and University education in the US, Russian Tours combines Western professionalism with the uniqueness of the customized tour guiding opportunities in St. Petersburg, Russia.
